    With the help of recommender systems

  • Participate in this user study and help us to build better recommendation systems.

    We ask you to do the following:

    • You are shown a list of 50 movies. Tell us which movies you like.

    • At the next page you get our list of recommended movies for you.
    • We ask you to select one single movie that you want to see next.

    • After having selected one movie, we will ask you a few questions.
    • If there are more movies that you find interesting and would like to watch in the future, you can add them to your wishlist.

    • We will additionally ask you a few questions about your general tastes afterwards and how you experienced the study
    • At the end of the study you get your Mechanical Turk Code
